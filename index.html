Our Covid-19 Policies

We understand these challenging times require new and unique solutions to continue offering quality service. Our goal and absolute standard is to assure all of our customers and employees are safe 100% of the time while providing the excellent service your used to. The following outlines our Corona-virus related policies:

  • All of our employees wear masks, gloves, and boot coverings while in contact with your vehicle.
  • Vehicle is sprayed with a fogging system with a disinfectant to ensure surface bacteria is eliminated upon completion of service.
  • Employees are required to be tested periodically to ensure the safety of our customers.
